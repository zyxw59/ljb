The consonants are shown on \cref{tab:consonants}. The sounds listed in
brackets only occur as allophones of other sounds:
\begin{itemize}
    \item The voiceless fricatives [ɸ θ x] occur as allophones of /β ð ɣ/
        before voiceless consonants.
    \item{} [ŋ] occurs as an allophone of /ɡ/ after vowels.
\end{itemize}

\section{Nouns}

Nouns in \Langname{} inflect for three cases -- nominative, accusative, and
locative -- and two numbers -- singular and plural.

There are five inflection paradigms, grouped into two declensions. The
different colors represent alternate stems.
